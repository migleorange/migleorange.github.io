Bachelor thesis

The purpose of this thesis is to research 3D scanning technology and build a workstation with already existing programs and equipment from Microsoft, Autodesk and Occipal. We will also compare other software and methods used for 3D scanning and its future. The final products is a working 3D scanning workstation for students and staff of Metropolia University of Applied Sciences.

  • Client: Helsinki University of Applied Sciences
  • Date: September 2015
  • Service: Research, 3D scanning
